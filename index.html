Me
Integrity
Exhibits respect, honesty and completeness in every interaction with others
Decisiveness
CORE: Makes decisions quickly, accurately and in the proper context
At Work
Confidence
CORE: Projects a healthy understanding and belief in one's own capabilities which in turn inspires trust in others
Leadership
Chooses appropriate styles and approaches when guiding people and/or initiatives in order to accomplish targeted goals and objectives
Client Focus
Understands and champions the needs and desires of the client when offering alternatives and making decisions
Detail Oriented
CORE: Pays attention to details and accounts for root causes rather than symptoms when solving problems and delivering solutions
Creative Problem Solving
Generates sound ideas and effective solutions that meet multiple needs using critical thinking skills.
Emotional Intelligence
Understands and manages one's emotions and gifts - as well as other's responses to them - in a way that motivates and engenders success
Initiative
CORE: Demonstrates an action-oriented willingness to take responsibility for resolving issues and completing work before being asked to do so
Responsiveness
CORE: Reacts quickly after accurately assessing the situation and clarifying expectations
Innovation
Solves problems or creates solutions that are fresh and unique
Stewardship
Plans time responsibly and uses resources optimally and in a sustainable fashion
Adaptability
CORE: Demonstrates flexibility in approach and style when conditions change
Influence
CORE: Creates a positive work environment and persuades others to willingly change
Empathy
CORE: Understands, respects and acts upon the feelings, concerns and perspectives of others
Knowledge Sharing
CORE: Understands and demonstrates that "knowledge is not power" by actively sharing knowledge, insights, obstacles, decisions and expectations with others in a timely and effective fashion.
Listening
Knows how to practice active listening. Allows others to talk and discuss ideas before forming opinions or making assumptions.
Communication
Delivers clear, concise, and accurate written and verbal communication
Negotiation
Builds consensus through accurate portrayal of capabilities, realistic commitments and by setting acceptable expectations in light of client's real needs
Relationship Building
CORE: Creates and nurtures working relationships that are beneficial to all parties
With Others
Collaboration
Seeks input from multiple sources and incorporates that input into a final decision or product
