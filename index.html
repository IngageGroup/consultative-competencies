Competencies for Consultants

Work with your coach to identify which competencies and mediums make the most sense:

Perseverance

Overcoming setbacks and disappointments to keep progressing towards the completion of a goal

Creativity

Breaking down complex issues and solving problems with both a fresh and a practical perspective.

Leadership

Chooses appropriate styles and approaches when guiding people and/or initiatives to accomplish targeted goals and objectives.

Initiative

Recognizing and doing what needs to be done before being asked to do it and expressing joy in each task as you give your best effort.

Confidence

Being self-assured based on past experience and success to help guide others to successful outcomes.

Wisdom

Seeing and responding to situations from a humble perspective that trascends my current circumstances - understanding the deeper reasons why things happen.

Responsibility

Knowing and doing what is expected of me by investing the time and energy to complete each task.

Flexibility

Willingness to change plans or ideas without getting upset to meet short term goals or deadlines.

Adaptability

Ability to adjust to new situations, environments and still perform at a high level over the long term.
